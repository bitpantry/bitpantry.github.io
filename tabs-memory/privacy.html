Privacy Policy

Effective date: May 1, 2026

Tabs Memory ("the App") is developed and operated by BitPantry. This policy describes how the App handles your information.

Data We Collect

The App does not collect, track, or share any personal data, analytics, or device identifiers. No third-party analytics or advertising SDKs are included in the App.

Account & Cloud Backup

If you choose to back up your cards to the cloud, you sign in with your Google account. When you do so, we receive a unique identifier and your email address from Google solely to authenticate you and associate your backup data with your account. Your verse cards and related content are stored on servers operated by BitPantry.

We do not sell, share, or use this information for any purpose other than providing the backup and sync service. You may delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the address below.

Data Stored on Your Device

All verse cards, tags, and progress data are stored locally on your device. If you do not use the cloud backup feature, no data leaves your device.

Third-Party Services

The only third-party service used is Google Sign-In for authentication. Google's privacy policy is available at policies.google.com/privacy.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.

Contact

Questions or requests regarding your data can be sent to support@bitpantry.net.
